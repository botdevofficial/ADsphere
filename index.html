Community Guidelines

To ensure a fair and safe environment for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

  • No Illegal Content: Promoting content that is illegal, hateful, or explicit is strictly forbidden and will result in a permanent ban.
  • Stay for Rewards: For "Join Channel" tasks, you must remain in the channel for the specified duration (e.g., 4 days) to keep your reward. Leaving early results in a penalty.
  • Fair Play: Do not use scripts, bots, or any other means to automate task completion. All tasks must be completed manually.
  • One Account Per User: Creating multiple accounts to exploit rewards is not allowed.
